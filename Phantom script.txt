I use starting out in Web3, I'm wondering which wallet to use. Let's talk Phantom. If I'm being very honest, I mostly use metamask. Phantom isn't my go-to wallet, but I've tried it out a bit, and here's my take. Phantom is very simple and beginner-friendly, especially on mobile. All you have to do is to sign in with your email and password, and you are in. You can use it to store crypto assets, interact with DApps, and explore NFTs. The interface is clean, lightweight, and much less overwhelming than some other wallets. Well, compared to MetaMask, I think Phantom is easier for someone who is just starting out. It has fewer clicks, fewer menus, and it's just straightforward. I haven't used it extensively, so I can't speak for everything, but from my first impression, it's solid. So if you're new to Web3, Phantom is a great option to explore.
